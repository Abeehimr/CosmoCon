Line Following Robot

Navigate. Automate. Dominate.

About the Competition

The Line Following Robot competition challenges teams to design, build, and program autonomous robots capable of following a designated path with speed and precision. This event tests your knowledge of sensors, control systems, programming logic, and mechanical design.

Robots will navigate through a complex track featuring straight lines, curves, intersections, and obstacles. The fastest robot to complete the course without losing the line wins. This competition emphasizes both hardware engineering and software optimization.

Competition Rules

  • Teams must consist of 2 to 4 members
  • Robot must be fully autonomous (no remote control)
  • Maximum robot dimensions: 25cm x 25cm x 25cm
  • Maximum robot weight: 2 kg
  • Power supply must be onboard (batteries only)
  • Robot must follow a black line on a white surface
  • Track width: 2.5cm (¬±0.5cm tolerance)
  • Robots must start from a stationary position
  • Timing stops when the robot crosses the finish line
  • Each team gets 3 attempts; best time counts
  • Leaving the track or stopping for more than 10 seconds results in disqualification for that round
  • Robots causing damage to the track will be disqualified
