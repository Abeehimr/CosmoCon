About CosmoCon'25

CosmoCon’25 is a hands-on tech event built for people who like building, breaking, and competing. It brings together coders, designers, and robotics enthusiasts for a day of challenges that reward skill, creativity, and smart thinking.

The event is divided into four focused modules, each designed to test a different side of tech — from pure logic to real-world hardware control. Whether you prefer solving problems on a screen or racing robots on the floor, CosmoCon’25 gives you a place to prove it.
